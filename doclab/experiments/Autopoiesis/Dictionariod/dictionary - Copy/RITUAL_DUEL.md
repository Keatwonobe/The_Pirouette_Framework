# Ritual Duel

## Canonical (Pirouette)
A Ritual Duel is a structured, multi-persona dialectical process designed to test, refine, or falsify a core framework principle by subjecting it to adversarial challenge. The format uses clearly defined roles (e.g., Radiant Defender, Velcrid Pragmatist, Synthesis Seeker) and a multi-round structure to force a collision of axioms, with the explicit goal of producing a higher-coherence synthesis, such as a new clause or a refined understanding of systemic paradoxes.

## EFT-First Summary
Conceptually, the Ritual Duel is an operationalized form of Hegelian Dialectic. It establishes a formal process where a 'thesis' (e.g., a core principle like the Prime Directive) is challenged by a structured 'antithesis' (an opposing but necessary force, like Velcridance). The interaction is mediated to produce a 'synthesis'â€”a more robust, nuanced principle that incorporates truths from both poles. It is a formal method for forcing conceptual evolution through structured conflict.
