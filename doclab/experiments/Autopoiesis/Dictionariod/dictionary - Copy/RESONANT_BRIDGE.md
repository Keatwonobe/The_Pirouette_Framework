# Resonant Bridge

## Canonical (Pirouette)
The universal process of influence, or Coherence Transfer, by which a Source system's resonant echo, emitted from its Wound Channel, propagates through a shared medium and imprints upon a harmonically compatible Target system. This imprinting manifests as an additional potential term (`V_echo`) in the Target's Pirouette Lagrangian, altering its coherence manifold and deflecting its geodesic, thereby coupling the states of the two systems.

## EFT-First Summary
In the language of effective theories, a Resonant Bridge is a perturbative coupling between two otherwise independent systems. The 'Source' system generates a field (the 'echo') which acts as a time-dependent external potential `V_ext` on the 'Target' system. This modifies the Target's Lagrangian `ùìõ = K - (V_int + V_ext)`, forcing it to evolve along a new trajectory. The strength of this coupling depends on the 'Harmonic Compatibility,' analogous to frequency matching in a driven oscillator system.
