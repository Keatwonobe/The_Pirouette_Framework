Replace the laboratory interval $\Delta t$ in Eq. A1 by the **observer-independent** proper interval $\Delta\tau$:

$$
\boxed{\;

      \Bigl|
      \frac{1}{\Delta\tau}\!
      \int_{0}^{\Delta\tau}\!\!

               \rho(0)\,
               U(\tau)\rho(0)U^{\dagger}(\tau)
             \bigr]
      d\tau
      \Bigr|^{2}
\;}
\tag{B1}
$$
