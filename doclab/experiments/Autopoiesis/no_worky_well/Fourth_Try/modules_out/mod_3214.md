* $T_a$ — dimensionless.
* $\dot T_a$ — s$^{-1}$.
* $\Gamma$ — N m² kg$^{-2}$.
* $g$ — pure number.
  The product $g\,\Gamma\,\dot T_a$ carries N m² kg$^{-2}$ s$^{-1}$.
  Inside the action density it multiplies **$A_\mu A^\mu$** (V²), giving J m$^{-3}$ as required.  The “undefined-units” criticism disappears.
