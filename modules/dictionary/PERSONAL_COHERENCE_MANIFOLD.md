# Personal Coherence Manifold

## Canonical (Pirouette)
The Personal Coherence Manifold is the N-dimensional topological space representing the total set of potential life-paths available to an individual. It is dynamically shaped by personal history (Wound Channels), values (coherence attractors, `K_œÑ`), and environmental constraints (pressure potentials, `V_Œì`). An individual's actual life is a geodesic on this manifold, a trajectory that locally maximizes the integrated Pirouette Lagrangian (`‚à´ ùìõ_p dt`).

## EFT-First Summary
The Personal Coherence Manifold is conceptually analogous to a potential energy landscape in condensed matter physics. An individual's life is a trajectory that seeks to maximize a "coherence potential" (`K_œÑ`), rather than minimize energy, subject to constraints. Unchosen life paths exist as alternative, accessible basins in this landscape, which can be explored via small, targeted perturbations known as Resonance Bridges.
